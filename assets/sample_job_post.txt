Customer Success Operations Specialist

We are hiring a Customer Success Operations Specialist to improve onboarding, reporting, and internal workflows for a growing SaaS team. The role partners with customer success managers, product operations, and support leads to make customer handoffs smoother and more measurable.

Responsibilities
- Maintain CRM data quality and customer lifecycle fields.
- Analyze customer onboarding metrics, support trends, and renewal risk indicators.
- Create process documentation, enablement materials, and customer-facing templates.
- Coordinate cross-functional projects that improve customer experience.
- Build weekly reports for leadership on onboarding progress and retention signals.
- Support customer success managers with account preparation and operational follow-up.

Requirements
- Experience in customer success operations, support operations, or SaaS operations.
- Strong spreadsheet skills and comfort with CRM systems.
- Ability to translate messy operational information into clear workflows.
- Excellent written communication and stakeholder management.
- Bonus: SQL, dashboard reporting, lifecycle automation, or product operations experience.
